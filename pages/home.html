Our Journey

LOYAL Rescue Inc. is a foster-based rescue and we do not operate a shelter. We do not have viewing hours as each pet is in a private home. Should you be the successful applicant for this pet, you are then welcome to come and meet the dog. You are not obligated to adopt, should you determine the fit is not correct, however we must go through this process prior to meeting the dog.

The Loyal Rescue team specailizes in caring for dogs. We make it our goal to find each dog that we aquire a loving home that fits them perfectly. Our team is able to identify which homes will be the best fit for our dogs. We are also very skilled at making sure the fogs we take in are ready for a new home with new people, because a lot of the dogs need a push and proper attention to make sure that they are ready for that step.
